Instructions:

Welcome to Piano Tiles! There are four different game modes to our special edition of Piano Tiles. All game modes consist of 30 rows of tiles. Use the "fghj" keys or click each black tile to play.

Classic Mode: Starting at the bottom, click/press all of the black tiles while avoiding all of the white tiles. More tiles will appear as you click/press them.

Double Mode: Press every PAIR of black tiles while avoiding all of the white tiles.

Letters Mode: Press all of the black tiles according to the letter indicated on the tile. Pressing any other letter will result in a loss.

Switch Mode: Click/press all of the black tiles while avoiding all of the white tiles. HOWEVER, every time you press a black tile, the next black tile has a chance to switch to a different column, so watch out!

Aim for the lowest times possible and have fun!
